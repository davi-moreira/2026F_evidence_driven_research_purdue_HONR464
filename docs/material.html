Core Course References

  • Blair, G., Coppock, A., & Humphreys, M. (2023). Research Design in the Social Sciences: Declaration, Diagnosis, and Redesign. Princeton University Press. Read free online: book.declaredesign.org.
  • Bergstrom, C. T., & West, J. D. (2020). Calling Bullshit: The Art of Skepticism in a Data-Driven World — optional companion; public case studies at callingbullshit.org.
  • Course datasets ship from the MIT-licensed rdss package (see notebooks/data/).
